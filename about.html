About Us

Al-Ameen Institute Of Information Sciences

Al-Ameen Institute Of Information Sciences, Bangalore also known as AIIS BENGALURU is a private college affiliated to BCU BENGALURU situated in Bengaluru, Karnataka. It was established in the year 1996 to provide higher education to build a career in the field of Computer Applications.Al-Ameen Institute Of Information Sciences is approved by the reputed AICTE, accrediated by NAAC.


Mumtaz ahmed Khan is the founder of Al-Ameen Educational Society. At the age of 31, in 1966 Khan founded al-Ameen movement. Number of social workers supported him gto start and establish around 20 branches of Al-ameen Educational Society and 200 Institutions throughout India. Mumtaz Ahmed Khan (6th september 1935-28th may 2021) was an humanitariun, educationalist and social reformer. Khan earned a MBBS degree from Madras University, chennai and became a general practitioner.
